The city of exile

Creation date: 26 september 2022

Short description

The 'City of Exile' was a project for a course at DAE, where participants were given two keywords and tasked with creating a game based on them. This game features a leveling system across various skills and immerses players in a story where they have been banished from their city. It adopts a dungeon runner style gameplay.
