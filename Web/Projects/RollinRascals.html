Rollin' rascals

Creation date: 1 march 2023

Short description

Step into the adorable world of our game, where you become a baby racing inside a hamster ball. The road ahead is full of obstacles that you need to overcome if you want to stay on the track above the clouds.

Game projects

This course marked our initial experience working as a team, which involved creating schedules and estimating tasks. Our team dynamic consisted of one programmer (myself), three artists, and one sound designer. Consequently, all the game logic you encounter in the project was developed by me, with the exception of audio-related aspects. The course required a commitment of 10 hours per week and spanned 11 weeks in total.
