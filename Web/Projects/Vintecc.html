Vintecc

Student job: 2022 (3 weeks), 2023 (8 weeks)

Short description

Vintecc is a software company that focuses on machine software with an expertise in vision, robotics and controls. We solve the major challenge of managing machines on a larger scale with our own developed machine management framework, which makes use of high-end technology such as Digital Twins, Big data, Artificial Intelligence, Web-based Interfaces and hardware independent software.

What did you do

My first year at Vintecc involved creating a program that facilitated data communication between C++, C++, or Python, utilizing shared memory and local networking. During this time, I conducted extensive benchmarking to determine if it could potentially replace the current system.

The second year involved exploring and conducting limit tests on various physics engines. The goal was to explore alternative physics options, and my role was to test and verify if they met our expectations. I worked with Mujoco and Havok, thoroughly testing both options, each with its own set of pros and cons. Unfortunately, I am unable to share any results due to legal restrictions.
